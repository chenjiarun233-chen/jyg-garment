Our Story

As a 3rd-gen merchant family, we know what it takes to build a brand that lasts — just like my grandpa did when he opened his shop in 1973. Ming Lang Ge (MLG) was established in 1998 by my father. We have been around the garment industry for about 30 yrs.

JYG

The year was 2025. We had a bold thought: What if we keep luxury-level quality for that premium vibe, but price is something all entrepreneurs with passion and bold ideas can afford? So they can easily build their own shops with their brand, no more dealing with unclear pricing and professional photography? That's how our international branch JYG began.
